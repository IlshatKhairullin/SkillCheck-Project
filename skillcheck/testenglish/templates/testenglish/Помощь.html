Useful information

The site in the upper left corner says which page you are on. To navigate through the site, use the navigation buttons in the top pinned menu. To start taking tests of various levels, click on the corresponding button in the side menu on the left. To determine your level, you need to pass the "Yourself" test, which is located on the HOME home page After passing the test, you will be asked to save the result, for this you will need to register on our website and log in. There are special sections for this, such as: LOGIN AND REGISTER

The questions are based on the school-preschool program For each question, you need to choose 1 answer option. Also below will be listed sites with useful information that will help you in the learning process.

If you have any questions or technical problems with the site, contact one of the employees. Their contacts are listed at the bottom on all pages of the site.
